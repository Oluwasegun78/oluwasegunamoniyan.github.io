Research Areas

My research examines variation in Nigerian English and other West African Englishes, focusing on sociophonetics and multimodal communication.

Nigerian English

Segmental and prosodic patterns, variation, and sociolinguistic meaning.

Sociophonetics

Vowels, formant dynamics, timing, and social patterning in speech.

Gesture & Deixis

How gesture and deictic expressions shape interpretation in interaction.

Speech Technology

Bias in forced alignment and recognition for underrepresented accents.

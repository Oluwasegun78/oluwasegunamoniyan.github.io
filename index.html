Research Areas

My research examines patterns of variation in Nigerian English and other West African Englishes, with a focus on sociophonetics and multimodal meaning.

  • Nigerian English phonetics and phonology
  • African Englishes and World Englishes
  • Sociophonetic variation (vowels, prosody, gesture)
  • Language ideology and speech technology bias
